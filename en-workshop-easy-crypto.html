Cryptography IS NOT anonymity

  • In WhatsApp, Messenger or Telegram, the content of the message or chat is encrypted. But the metadata (who you are talking with, how often and when) are accessible in clear.
  • Metadata is sometime enough to reveal your identity as in the famous journalistic scandal Les fadettes du Monde

Anonymity

  • Create and use a dead drop, disconnected from your identity
  • Take advantage of disposable emails such as mailinator, yopmail, dispatchable, mailcatch, etc.
  • Send self destructed notes as in Mission: impossible using Privnote or Zerobin
  • Work collaboratively AND encrypt your documents with CryptPad (can also be used on the same principle as dead drop)

Encryption

  • Encrypt your emails with Proton Mail or Tutanota
  • Use Signal on your smartphone AND on your computer
  • You are using another application? Discord, WhatsApp, iMessage, etc.? Check the safety of your app.
Insta messages are not end-to-end encrypted. Only from the smartphone to the Meta servers (lol that shitty name) then from the server to the recipient's smartphone

Encryption AND Anonymity

On your smartphone, install Wire or Element. None of these applications require a phone number to create an account (unlike Signal).
